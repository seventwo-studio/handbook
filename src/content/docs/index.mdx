## About SevenTwo Studio

We're SevenTwo Studio, a product studio focused on building consumer apps that make life simpler. We also create developer tools—APIs and platforms—that power the next generation of products. Everything we ship gives people new capabilities without the complexity.

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Engineering" icon="rocket">
    Developer velocity, pragmatic solutions, and technical excellence. We maintain strict code quality standards, uncompromising git workflows, and balance simple foundations with modern scalability.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Product" icon="star">
    Design-driven, user-centric approach. We build multiple products simultaneously, ship small frequent releases, and never compromise on UX quality and craft.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Operations" icon="setting">
