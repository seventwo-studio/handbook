The SevenTwo Studio Handbook is the single source of truth for how we build software. Whether you're joining a project, starting your own, or learning how a professional engineering team operates — this is where the standards live.

It covers language conventions, app architecture patterns, and engineering practices. Everything you need to write code the SevenTwo way.

## Two Ways to Use This Handbook

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="I'm learning" icon="open-book">
    New to this codebase or to professional development in general? Follow the [Learning Path](/start-here/first-day/) — it walks you through setup, your first PR, your first feature, and beyond. Then use [Pick Your Stack](/start-here/pick-your-stack/) to find the guides relevant to what you're building.
  </Card>

  <Card title="I need a quick answer" icon="magnifier">
    Already familiar with the codebase and need to look something up? Jump straight to the [Reference](/reference/languages/) for consolidated rule checklists, or browse the guides for in-depth explanations and code examples.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
